Our Story

Every Fla vor Tells a Story

Nestled in the heart of Galle Fort, Pedlers Inn Café offers a unique blend of Sri Lankan flavors and international dishes, served in a charming, colonial-style setting. Established with a passion for great food and a love for heritage, we invite guests to savor a delightful culinary experience amidst the rich history of Galle. Whether you're here for a quick coffee, a hearty meal, or a leisurely afternoon, our welcoming atmosphere and exceptional service ensure that every visit is memorable. At Pedlers Inn Café, tradition meets taste, offering a truly unforgettable dining experience.
